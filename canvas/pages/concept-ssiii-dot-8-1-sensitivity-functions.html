Topic 8: Frequency Domain Design

Concept 8.1: Sensitivity Functions

Control systems affect the dynamics of a system in multiple ways. Broadening our thinking of a process system from setpoint following to also include measurement noise and external disturbances, we can now analyse the way that the controller interact with these effects. This leads us to the canonical “Gang of Six” transfer functions which describe the behaviour of a SISO system in full generality.
