Concept 7.4: Integrator Windup

In constrained systems, a mismatch can arise between the integral component of the control signal and the physical output of an actuator — if the actuator is already working at 100%, it is counterproductive to keep accumulating the error signal. In this concept, we illustrate the issues that “Integrator Wind-Up” causes and some strategies to avoid them.
