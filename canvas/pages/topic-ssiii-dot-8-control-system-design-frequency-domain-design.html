  Topic 8: Frequency Domain Design

This topic will cover more detailed concepts in our control system design approaches. As more complex systems are considered, a range of iterative approaches for designing higher order controllers are possible — this is known as Loop Shaping. These factors involve the sensitivity of a controller to changes in the environment (a.k.a. its robustness) and the trade-offs necessary between achieving good performance and good robustness.
