Topic 5: Transfer Functions

Concept 5.1: Frequency Domain Modeling

This concept introduces the Transfer Function as an alternative to state-space models for representing a dynamical system. Unlike the general state-space approach, a transfer function represents a single-input-single-output, linear, system only. It allows systems to be considered in terms of their frequency response — their gain and phase as a function of frequency given sinusoidal inputs. This perspective provides a series of tools and visualisations for control systems design.
