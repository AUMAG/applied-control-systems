Concept 9.3: System Design Considerations

In this concept we jump ahead to Chapter 14 to discuss the implication of non-minimum phase dynamics on control system design — these generally apply substantial limits on achievable performance. We also consider the conditions under which an unstable system can be stabilised by a stable controller ("strong stabilisability"), and the limitation on performance that results from the "waterbed effect" from Bode's Integral Formula.
