Concept 3.4: Linearisation

This is the final concept for this topic. In this concept, two linearisation techniques for nonlinear systems are introduced: Jacobian Linearisation, and Feedback Linearisation. Jacobian Linearisation is robust but is only valid around the point chosen to linearise at. Feedback Linearisation is a general approach, but is not robust to unmodelled dynamics or measurement errors.
