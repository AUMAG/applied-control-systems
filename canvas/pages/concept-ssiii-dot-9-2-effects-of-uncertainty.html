Concept 9.2: Effects of Uncertainty

Uncertainty in the model can affect both performance and stability. We define robustness as the measure by which a control system remains stable and performant in the presence of uncertainty. In this concept, this is linked explicitly to the sensitivity function from the Gang of Six. We leave discussion of robust design techniques (LQR, H-infinity, etc.) to a more advanced course.
