Concept 2.1: Solving Differential Equations

In this concept we use (a little) more formal mathematics to define differential equations and discuss various solution types which are possible. This allows us to consider stability and instability, broadening slightly to mention weird cases like non-unique solutions and ‘finite escape time’ solutions.
