Concept 5.5: Zero Frequency Gain, Poles, and Zeros

In this concept, a number of critical ideas are introduced, including transfer function gain at zero and high frequencies, and the meanings and definitions of poles and zeros. We define the number of excess poles, proper and strictly proper systems, and see the pole-zero map (pzmap). The idea of pole-zero cancellation is briefly discussed in the context of the standard control systems block diagram.
