Concept 5.6: The Bode Plot

This is the final concept for this rather lengthy topic — thanks for your hard work to get through it this week. In this concept, we formally introduce the Bode plot as a tool for visualising the various properties of a transfer function. This includes a discussion of non-minimum phase systems, time delays, low- and high-pass filters, and how the frequency response can be measured experimentally and used to estimate the transfer function of a system.
