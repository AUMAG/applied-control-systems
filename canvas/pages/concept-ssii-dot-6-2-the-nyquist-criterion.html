Concept 6.2: The Nyquist Criterion

You have seen the Bode Plot — now introducing the Nyquist Plot. These are used primarily for control system design to predict and understand the stability and the performance of a loop transfer function. This concept introduces and then demonstrates an example of the Nyquist Stability Criterion.
