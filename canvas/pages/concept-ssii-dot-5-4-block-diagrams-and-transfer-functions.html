Concept 5.4: Block Diagrams and Transfer Functions

Throughout this course to date, we have seen examples of block diagrams used to illustrate the structure of dynamical and control systems. In this concept, block diagrams are introduced formally, including block diagram algebra that allows direction manipulation of a block diagram for simplification and analysis. The canonical block diagrams of control systems are discussed in this context.
