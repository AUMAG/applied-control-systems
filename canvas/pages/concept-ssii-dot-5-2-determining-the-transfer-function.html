Topic 5: Transfer Functions [continued]

In this concept, we introduce the exponential signal, exp(st), where s is the complex frequency. Using s, we see examples of common transfer functions including the standard mass-spring-damper second order system, as well as the structure of a generalised transfer function. We define the important terms poles and zeros, which relate in part to the eigenvalues of the system, and see how to calculate a transfer function based on the state matrices.
