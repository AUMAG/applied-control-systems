  Topic 7: PID Control

The PID Controller is the most common control system implemented in practice, and it provides a surprisingly powerful set of capabilities for controlling most dynamical systems encountered in practice. This topic reiterates the standard form and components of PID and demonstrates

  • the utility of this simple controller even when used to control complex plants,
  • a variety of methods to approach PID tuning problems, and 
  • a series of practical considerations.
