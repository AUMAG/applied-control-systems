This concept is the crux of the topic. Having now discussed solution types and qualitative analysis, you will first consider stability from a more formal mathematical sense (‘stability in the sense of Lyapunov’). Then one of the all time key points: we will link the solution of systems of linear differential equations with the eigenvalues of their state matrices. (Oh no, eigenvalues!) Finally, we will see how these concepts can be applied to nonlinear systems via approximation or more formal mathematical analysis.
