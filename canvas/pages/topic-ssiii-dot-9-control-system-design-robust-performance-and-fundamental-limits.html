Module III: Control System Design (Topics 7–9)

This third and final module will provide you with insight into key control system design principles. The PID Controller is covered in detail, followed by more general frequency domain-based principles. Finally, the course concludes with a summary of robustness and control system limitations.

  Topic 9: Robust Performance & Fundamental Limits

In the final topic of the course, we combine elements of several previous topics by analysing the performance of robust controllers due to various factors, in particular measurement noise and external disturbances. These analyses allow us to draw conclusions on the fundamental limitations of linear control systems, and indicate directions of improvement that may be possible by considering various types of nonlinear control systems.
