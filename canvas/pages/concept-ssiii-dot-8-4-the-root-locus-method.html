Concept 8.4: The Root-Locus Method

In the interests of time we skip the textbook section on "Feedforward Design". We conclude this topic with a discussion of the complementary approach to control systems design using "root locus" , which considers the explicit locations of the closed loop eigenvalues of the system and how they move with changing gain.
