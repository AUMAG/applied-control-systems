Topic 9: Robust Performance & Fundamental Limits

Concept 9.1: Modeling Uncertainty

We have discussed the idea of control system robustness to changing dynamics. In this concept, we consider explicit models of uncertainty as part of our block diagram. These include parametric uncertainty (where the true parameters of the system might not match the model) and unmodelled dynamics (where the model has a lower dynamic order than the true dynamics). We briefly consider the use of the Vinnicombe metric for quantitatively assessing how similar two transfer functions are.
