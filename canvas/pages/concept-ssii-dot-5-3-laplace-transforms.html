Concept 5.3: Laplace Transforms

In this concept we sketch out the Laplace Transform and its use as a powerful tool for analytically solving ODEs. In this course we do not require extensive working knowledge of these analytical procedures, preferring to solve systems numerically; nonetheless, we make extensive use of the concepts of the Laplace Transform and it's important to recognise the fundamental relationships between s-domain poles and zeros and time-domain differential equation coefficients.
