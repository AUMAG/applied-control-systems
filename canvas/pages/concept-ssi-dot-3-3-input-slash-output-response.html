Concept 3.3: Input / Output Response

In this concept, we use the convolution equation to demonstrate the general solution of a linear-time-invariant system of equations. We demonstrate that the dynamic properties of a system are invariant to coordinate transformations, then detail more thoroughly the step and sinusoidal inputs and their standardised outputs in terms of transient response, steady-state response, and frequency response.
