Topic 6: Frequency Domain Analysis

Concept 6.1: The Loop Transfer Function

When designing control systems, properties of the controlled system can be predicted from the open loop dynamics of the controller and the plant in series. This is known as the Loop Transfer Function and is used for assessing and understanding stability and performance. This concept introduces the stability criterion and demonstrates with an example how a control system with a time delay can become unstable even if the underlying dynamics of the system are stable.
