Module I: Dynamical Systems  (Topics 1–3)

Over three topics, this module will generalise concepts from Engineering Mechanics and Dynamics into the study of Dynamical Systems.

  Topic 2: Dynamic Behaviour

The second topic in this Module will provide a background in understanding dynamical systems — approaches to finding their solutions and understanding their behaviour.

This will provide the foundation for understanding and analysing control systems.
