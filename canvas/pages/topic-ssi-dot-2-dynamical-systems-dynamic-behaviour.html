  Topic 2 conclusion

After reviewing the concepts above and completing the activities, you should now:

  • Be able to use Matlab to solve differential equations with solvers such as ode45
  • Be able to use Matlab to visualise the properties of simple dynamical systems using the quiver plot and the phase portrait
  • Understand the concept of and identify equilibria and stability in simple dynamical systems
  • Follow through examples of analysing dynamical systems across different engineering disciplines
