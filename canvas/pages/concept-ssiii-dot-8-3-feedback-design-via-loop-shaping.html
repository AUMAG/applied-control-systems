Concept 8.3: Feedback Design via Loop Shaping

We have now seen how all factors of control system performance are linked through the Gang of Six to the loop transfer function. Therefore, by carefully designing the loop transfer function will allow us to meet all desired specifications (within the limitations of the sensor and actuator system). This is known as "loop shaping"; this concept discusses some common approaches used.
