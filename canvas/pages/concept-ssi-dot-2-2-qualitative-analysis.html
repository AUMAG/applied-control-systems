Concept 2.2: Qualitative Analysis

In this concept, you will learn the general ways that dynamical systems are visualised and discussed for the purposes of qualitative analysis. This allows you to broadly understand the behaviour of a dynamical system in terms of its equilibria and stability properties. Some examples from the literature are used to illustrate how limit cycles can arise in different systems.
