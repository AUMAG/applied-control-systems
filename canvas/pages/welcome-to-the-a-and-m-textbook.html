Course textbook

Textbook by Åström & Murray (Å&M)

BOOK Feedback Systems: An Introduction for Scientists and Engineers, Second Edition

Åström, Karl Johan ; Murray, Richard M., Princeton University Press, 2021
 View item
 

The course Applied Control Systems has been designed explicitly around this textbook, and we will be covering it chapter by chapter, section by section, each week. Areas in the text marked with dangerous bend symbols will often be overlooked, and Chapters 2, 8, & 15 are (mostly) omitted due to time constraints.

Online version of the text

As well as through the library, at time of writing the textbook is also available online through the publisher's website:

  Feedback Systems: An Introduction for Scientists and Engineers

Alongside PDF files for each chapter, this site also provides additional resources for each chapter such as example Python code, frequently asked questions, extra exercises, and additional resources.
